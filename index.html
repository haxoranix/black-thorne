The Hollow House

When investigative journalist Sarah Mills inherits a decrepit Victorian mansion, she thinks she's found the perfect story. But as she delves into the house's dark history, she realizes that some secrets are better left buried.

Strange whispers echo through the halls at night. Shadows move when no one is watching. And the previous owners all met untimely deaths. Sarah discovers that the house is a gateway between worlds, and something ancient and malevolent has been waiting for her arrival.
